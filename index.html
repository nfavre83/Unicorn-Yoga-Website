Welcome at Unicorn Yoga!

Yoga Classes

At Unicorn Yoga, we always strive to include various styles of yoga to our yoga practitioners. We propose for instance in our school premises:

  • Hatha yoga
  • Vinyasa yoga
  • Kundalini yoga
  • Iyengar yoga
  • Ashtanga yoga
  • Sivananda yoga
  • Aerial yoga
  • Yin yoga
  • Restorative yoga
  • Power yoga
  • Prenatal yoga
  • Yoga for babies and toddlers

Our Hatha and Power yoga classes are available on-site according to our school's timetable and online at your convenience.
